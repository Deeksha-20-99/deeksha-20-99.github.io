About

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Arizona State University, and a member of the ASU KIM Lab. My research focuses on agentic AI for human-in-the-loop cyber-physical systems, with an emphasis on deterministic AI, real-time systems, and the integration of large language models in safety-critical environments.

I hold a Master’s degree in Robotics and Autonomous Systems (Artificial Intelligence) from Arizona State University, and a Bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from BMS Institute of Technology and Management.
